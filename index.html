VISION & MISSION

Our Collaborative TradeTM model takes direct and fair trade one step further. Coffee is an opportunity to do good,and doing good means we get to support whole communities to make positive change. Since 2007,Land of a thousand hills Coffee has worked with farmers in Rwanda as co-labourars creating a beater future through coffee.We build flourishing communities through holistic solutions, seeking to meet social,economic and spiritual needs in the most sustainable way possible with each Do Good Initiative Project developed and run by local leadership.
